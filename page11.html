VAWC

(November 26, 2018)

On this day, orange color is seen everywhere as we support our school's advocacy in ending Violence Against in Women and Children. This is an 18-day campaign to end Violence Against Women and Children (VAWC) is observed anually from November 25 to December 12. Its main goal is to raise awareness and protect the human rights of women and children.

PreviousNext

Our school held programs regarding this advocacy such as putting free wall where students could write their thoughts about VAWC. They also discussed about the campaign and lastly, they put fliers on the school's walls so students are aware of our school's part in campaigning this program.
