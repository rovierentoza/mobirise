English Month

( November 2018 )

Last November 23, 2018, Pasig City Science High School celebrated English month to show the importance of reading with the theme “Reading is the Key to a better future.” During the opening speech of our PCSHS OIC-Principal Charlie Fababaer, he said that “reading is a very important skill because this is how information about almost everything is acquired.”  


There are various competitions happened held by the English teachers such as declamation, spelling bee, speech choir and impromptu speech. Students who excelled with those competitions were awarded with certificates and medals. 


The most highlighted contest on this month was the winning performances of Grade 10 students of Miss Saigon on stage where the students showed their talent in acting and singing with emotion which really felt by the audience.
