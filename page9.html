QUANTUM

( October 5, 2018 )


Way back 13 years ago, Pasig City Science High School was built and established. The school is always a home for students who love studying math and science. Many students and teachers built their memories in this school's corridors, classrooms, libraries and many more.

13 years later, we are now bringing back the roots of our beloved school. As we celebrate the school's anniversary with the theme "Quantum: Bringing Back the Pascian Roots", with the help of SSG officers, and the staffs and in partnership with Hawk Bags, this important event was really successful.  

This year is very important to the Pascian community as we rise back from our dark ages. That's why the ssg and the administration put lots of effort in this year foundation day. 

The day started with a mass and worshipping ministry in order to thank God for bringing back Pasig City Science High School's glory. After the mass, our beloved OIC-Principal, Mr. Charlie Fababaer, had a speech on how we all strived fighting for our school and after that, he cut the ribbon as a sign of opening and starting the celebration. The booths were headed by each department and clubs. It was fun seeing teachers and students enjoy serving their customers with big smiles and good service.

All students have smiles on their faces while visiting each club. seeing all the happy, laughing, and satisfied faces of each student, teacher and visitors in pasig city science high school made me realize that this is a year to remember as we went back 13 years ago.

UPROAR

(October 5, 2018)

As a part of the celebration for our foundation day, an event that is headed by the SSG officers in partnership with Hawk Bags serenaded students, visitors and the teachers.

A night full of bands singing to the crowd. One of the most awaited event after 3 years happened. The Battle of the Bands!

This year's theme is "Uproar: Growl For Greatness". Many bands from different grade levels joined and showcased their talents in singing and playing musical instruments.

Many enjoyed the performances of the ten bands. That night was lit as the crowd were singing along with the performers. Many also had their cellphone's flashlight and waving them through the air as they feel the moment.

After all the bands have performed, a guest band came up to the stage. All students rushed to go in front to closely watch the performers. This band is called "This Band"! They performed songs and the crown was wild as they sang along throughout the whole performance. It was a wholesome experience.
