Outreach Program

( December 15, 2018 )

Christmas is truly a blessing from God, for we have been instruments to share and give back to the less fortunate on our recently concluded 4th PCSHS Outreach Program. Let's look back as we continue to live with the true values of this season.


PCSHS values their compassion for the unfortunate, and gave clothes, books and even toys. The event started with a welcome speech. All were given a gift from the school. It all ended with warm smiles and happy faces.
