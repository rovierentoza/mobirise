PCSHS SciOlympics

( September 28, 2018 )

PCSHS successfully launched the fourth Sci-Olympics in the school corresponding with our OIC-Principal Mr. Charlie Fababaer, Science Department had the SSG Officers last Sept 28, 2018. Everyone really enjoyed accomplishing all the tasks applying all their knowledge about different concepts in different branches of science.

There is also a Cookfest held where students' abilities in cooking and knowledege in Science collaborates. Each group was randomly composed of students from different grade level and has assigned teachers.
