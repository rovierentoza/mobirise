PCSHS Coalition

( March 9, 2019 )

PCSHS Coalition is composed of young dancers who are not only to showcase their talent but also to inspire those who doubt their ability in dancing. To excel in the academics is their commitment as students but to pursue their passion in dancing is their biggest aspiration in life. This group definitely proved that Pascians can be both intellectually gifted in school and at the same time incredibly talented on the dance floor. This is why they joined, for the very first time, a dance competition called “Dance Supremacy.” 



The group practiced for two months with the help of their coach Macky Quiobe from UPeepz and finally got the chance to perform on stage last March 9, 2019.
