TEACHER'S DAY

(October 5, 2018)

This is the most important day for all teachers who work hard 24/7 just to make sure students are learning and acquiring new knowledge. This is the day where we pay back our teachers with gratitude and love as they helped us mold ourselves and be better persons.

 This is the day where we show them how truly we appreciate their hard works and patience. This is October's World Teacher's Day!

SSG officers with the help of alumni members, made a wonderful celebration for our teachers. this year's theme is "Aeipathy: Teaching with a Heart, Soul, and Creativity".

the day started with students escorting their teachers to the gymnasium where they walk down on red carpet and treated them as royalties. the program started with a prayer where students give thanks to the Lord for having great teachers. after that, our OIC-Principal, Mr. Charlie Fababaer gave speech and talked about how hard the teachers fought for our school's rights. everyone cheered after that.

it was a magical moment as teachers laugh, enjoy and some cried as students give their gifts and letters to them. the alumni faculty also give back to their high school teachers their appreciation. they held a raffle contest and give gift checks to some teachers.

it is truly a great event as we look back to those days where our teachers become our friend, a parent, and most importantly, one of our inspiration.
